The Problem: Knowledge Silos

In large organizations, experts in specialized fields like are often unknown to their colleagues. This leads to expensive external training and wasted time.

SkillSwap AI was founded as part of the CP3402 course to bridge this gap. Our mission is to break down these barriers by providing an AI-driven directory that facilitates 1-on-1 knowledge transfers between coworkers.

Our Core Features

Intelligent Skill Mapping

Our AI scans internal profiles to identify core strengths and technical skills across your entire workforce.

Automated Mentorship

The algorithm automatically suggests the best person in the building to help you with your specific project hurdles.

Progress Tracking

HR teams gain insights into internal skill growth through comprehensive session logs and impact data.
